## Where It Started: A Street in Italy

"Pasta 25 euro? Bullshit!"

That was my honest reaction at a small *trattoria* on a Ligurian street corner.

But I ordered it. And when it arrived—a simple bowl of **Aglio e Olio** with warm bread under Mediterranean sun—I got it.

**The numbers:**
- Cost to make: €11-12
- Price I paid: €25
- Overpriced? No. Worth it? Yes.

**Why?** I wasn't buying pasta. I was buying *story*—30 years of the same chef doing the same thing the same way. The morning market. The sourced garlic. The stone buildings. The ease of a tradition that works.

I visit every year now. Same corner. Same dish. Same price. And every year, I understand a bit better: **price and cost are not the same thing.**

## My Philosophy: The Dual Role

I work on both sides of a negotiation table. I apply different logic to each. This isn't hypocrisy—it's **professional discipline.**

### When I'm Buying (Evaluating Contractors)

A subcontractor quotes RM 65K. I verify:

| Item | Quote | Market | Savings |
|------|-------|--------|---------|
| Labor (RM 35/hr) | RM 35K | RM 28-32/hr = RM 28K | RM 7K |
| Materials | RM 20K | RM 17K (bulk pricing) | RM 3K |
| Overhead | RM 10K | RM 7K (standard) | RM 3K |
| **Total** | **RM 65K** | **RM 52K** | **RM 13K** |

**I say:** "Fair price is RM 52K. Your quote is RM 13K above market. Either your efficiency is poor, or you're padding. Let's discuss."

**I'm ruthless.** No emotion. Pure cost verification.

### When I'm Selling (Quoting My Services)

I quote RM 65K for QS services. My cost is RM 40K (my time, overhead, risk). The difference—RM 25K (60% margin)—is justified by:

| Value Element | Justification |
|---|---|
| **RICS APC Credential** | 20+ years study + exam + professional standing |
| **20+ Years APAC Experience** | Depth, network, judgment you can't buy elsewhere |
| **Tools Mastery** | Primavera P6, RIB CostX, Power BI—rare combination |
| **Contract Fluency** | FIDIC, PAM, commercial law application |
| **Professional Insurance** | RM 5M liability coverage; if I miss a RM 500K claim, it falls on me |
| **Judgment Call** | When you need someone to say "yes" or "no" to a dispute—you're buying accountability |

**I say:** "This is the market rate for a RICS-qualified QS with my profile. If you want cheaper, you'll find someone with less experience or no insurance. That's a business decision."

**I build in value.** Transparently.
