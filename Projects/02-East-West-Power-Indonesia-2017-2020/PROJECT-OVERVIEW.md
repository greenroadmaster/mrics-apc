## Project Scale & Complexity

### Technical Scope
- Main equipment: Boiler (capacity 2,400 t/h), turbine (350MW), generator, cooling tower
- Electrical systems: 500kV transmission grid connection, substations
- Civil works: Foundation, buildings, roads, cooling water intake
- Environmental systems: Air pollution control, ash disposal, water treatment

### Commercial Complexity
- **Multi-party contracts:** Owner (KEPCO) ↔ EPC Contractor (Samsung C&T) ↔ Subcontractors/Vendors
- **Multi-currency:** USD + IDR
- **Government coordination:** Ministry of Energy, PLN (national utility), provincial government, customs
- **Supply chain:** Korean, Chinese, Italian equipment vendors across long lead times

### Timeline
- **2017 (Months 1–4):** Mobilization, site setup, local team recruitment
- **2018 (Months 5–16):** Major construction phase, civil & structural works
- **2019 (Months 17–28):** Equipment installation, system testing, pre-commissioning
- **2020 (Months 29–32):** Commissioning, grid connection, handover

## Evidence of Commercial Competency

### Monthly Claims Assessment
- **Frequency:** 10–12 progress claims per year
- **Amount per claim:** USD 15–30 million
- **Responsibility:** Verify quantities, assess rates, confirm entitlement, certify payment
- **Example (Jan 2019):** Samsung C&T submitted USD 28.5M progress claim
  - Civil works completion: 65% (measured by independent engineer)
  - Equipment delivery: 40% (boiler & turbine on-site)
  - Won's Assessment: Certified USD 26.8M (2% disputed items pending clarification)

### Variation Order Management
- **Total VOs during project:** ~8–10 major changes
- **Total VO value:** USD 15–20M
- **Types of changes:**
  - Design modifications (design responsibility: client risk)
  - Material cost escalation (2017–2019 market conditions)
  - Schedule acceleration (client request, time cost)
- **Example:** Design change for turbine cooling system = USD 2.5M cost, approved after technical evaluation

### Cost & Commercial Reporting
- **Frequency:** Monthly management reports to KEPCO HQ (Seoul)
- **Content:** Progress payment status, budget vs actual, commercial risks, claims pipeline
- **Audience:** Owner's Finance Director, Project Vice President, Internal Audit
