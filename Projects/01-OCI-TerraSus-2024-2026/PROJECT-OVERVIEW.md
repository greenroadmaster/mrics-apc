## Work Experience Narrative

### Cost Estimation & BOQ Preparation
> "Prepared detailed Bills of Quantities (BOQ) for all project packages using RIB CostX professional estimation software. Quantity take-off coordinated with design consultants (Juruthera Jasa) covering civil, architectural, MEP, and HVAC scopes. BOQ finalized and signed for tender release; process included scope clarification, site surveys, and cost benchmarking against market rates."

### Project Controls & Scheduling
> "Developed and maintained Primavera P6 master project schedule with WBS/OBS alignment. Set up cost baseline and assigned budget to 400+ activities. Monitored earned value metrics (BCWS, BCWP, ACWP, CPI, SPI) on monthly basis; produced EAC (Estimate at Completion) forecast for management review. Maintained risk register with probability/impact assessment; implemented mitigation and contingency plans as conditions evolved."

### Contract Administration
> "Administered 15+ subcontractor contracts under FIDIC and PAM conditions as employer's representative. Reviewed and certified monthly Interim Payment Certificates (IPC) — average 8 IPCs per month covering RM 4–6M in progress claims. Assessed contractor cost submissions for quantity accuracy, rate reasonableness, and contractual entitlement. Liaised with Independent Quantity Surveyor (IQS) for claim verification and dispute resolution."

### Cost Control & Financial Management
> "Maintained project cost database tracking all spending across 15+ subcontractors and vendors. Prepared monthly cost reports showing budget vs actual variance analysis, cash flow projection, and profit margin tracking. Managed subcontractor payment schedules and back-charges for cost recovery. Final forecast: RM 118.7M contract value, RM 101.8M cost of works, RM 16.9M net profit — maintained throughout project cycle."

### Variation Order Management
> "Processed approximately 30 variation orders totaling RM 5.2M contract sum adjustments (2024-2026). Evaluated cost and schedule impact of change instructions; assessed contractor quantum claims for reasonableness. Prepared detailed cost breakdowns; coordinated approval with client project management and independent QS. All VOs documented with supporting justification."

### Regulatory Approvals & Government Liaison
> "Obtained commencement permit from Majlis Bandaraya through direct liaison with local authority. Coordinated with Jabatan Bomba dan Penyelamat Malaysia (JBPM) for fire safety approval; reviewed contractor fire-rated panels, suppression systems, and alarm specifications for compliance. Processed CIDB, SIRIM, and NPRA certifications for imported materials; managed customs clearance and utility permits (TNB/Sarawak Energy). Obtained police authority permits for abnormal load transport."

### Project Closeout & Completion
> "Coordinated final inspection and handover process. Obtained Certificates of Practical Completion (CPC) for two major contract packages. Managed Defects Liability Period (DLP) with snag list inspection and contractor remediation. Final account settlement in progress (expected completion Q3 2026)."
